Effect of videogames in people

Technology is growing by leaps and bounds, then, videogames are not the exception at all. Every day more and more people are getting involved in new experiences related with technology by diverse reasons, some people for money, some for simple hobby and even for earnings by social media and/or streaming on live. Furthermore, streaming has become one of the most popular entertainment media all around the world in which gamers obtain even more profits than in a professional job.

However, people considered only viewers spend most of their spare time just supporting streamers, effect that has caused resection of physical and professional performance in teens and young adult people. Video gaming is a good entertainment and distress manner. However, it can become dangerous for people with no strong mindset for real life, almost comparable as drugs. Videogames and streamers have caught such high attention from viewers that they become addicts to it. Since many years ago, gamers and viewers addict pay more attention to these devices than their own health, their own family, and their own education and/or jobs.

In addition, following the rhythm in which streamers are growing in amount, in a very near future there will be more streamers on Earth than white-collar professional workers, being of course the youth generations the majority range age for these activities. And what it is even more surprising is that they will keep earning more than older population who keeps working at their offices during their entire life. Nevertheless, the possible consequences are not very clear yet; there are a huge number of opinions and theories about how the future will be in a world even more surrounded by technology, a future in which human beings will become unsociable species only paying attention to the screens in front of their eyes, just like many movies have warned us.
