Introduction

I am not a psychologist, but like you, who are readding this, I have also had tough seasons in my life, just like everyone in this planet. Seasons and incidents that made me feel low, like proffesionally unexperienced, almost a child compared to other's talent and skills, I have also been rejected in many jobs I felt so enthusiastic about, I have also failed many some university subjects though time and many failures more in my life. I have felt like just giving up. But I also have learned that throwing everything away is never the solution. It is an answer, yes, but it is not the correct one. The following words are the reflection of my personal thoughts after years and years of educational and professional efforts in my country (Mexico), and even here in Canada so far.

Motivation

We are living tough times, inflation, wars, debts, unemployment and insecurity in many countries, pollution, and many more situations that each one of us must deal with every day. We start doubting on ourselves when desperation and stress eat us alive, most of times we just feel like quit and get away from these difficult situations without looking back ever again. The world is full of negative situations, but it is just us who decide how to react to these situations. We are human beings, it is in our nature to fail, to self-doubting, to decrease our hopes, to question why things happened that way and to us. The world has made us believe that we must not make mistakes in professional and sometimes educational fields to achieve a well-positioned jobs and to place national and international companies in the top against competitors. However, even the toughest situations have a solution, and that is our minds, our self-respect, self-love.

Happiness is a decision. We feel low, sadness and even paranoia when facing difficult situations, but we are not alone. We have our family, our friends to encourage us to keep going, we have to each other. Life is made of challenges and we all shall know that we came to this world not to overcome others but overcome ourselves. By challenging ourselves we grow as persons make us more grateful beings, and that is what our parents teach us since childhood, but we start forgetting while growing up. When we find what we really love to do, we star enjoying life. Life begins when we stop considering other people's negative opinions and learning from our mistakes to start again now from experience. When we accept that mistakes are not bad things our mindsets strengthen and give us the courage to face difficulties with a smile and do whatever you once thought you could not. There will be many moments in which we are going to feel low and defeated again, but if you could rise your head once before, you can do it any other time in the future no matter the edge, no matter the situation, no matter the cost.

Success is when you overcome difficulties by knowing you never gave up when that was exactly what you wanted the most during that process. If you fail, that is all right as long as you keep trying again and again after feeling defeated and keep trying until you make it.
